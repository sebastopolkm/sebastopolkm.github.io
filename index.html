Kalyana Mitta

Our Kalyana Mitta (Pali for Spiritual Friends) group is a peer facilitated group that started in 2006 in Sebastopol. We follow the process of Dharma Contemplation, developed by Gregory Kramer as part of his Insight Dialogue program. It is a way of coming together and deeply reflecting on a passage of the Buddhaâ€™s words from the Suttas. We meditate for 15 minutes before and after the process, and do a brief personal check-in at the end. After the meeting we then have an optional gathering for tea, snacks, and socializing.
